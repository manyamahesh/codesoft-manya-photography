About Me

I’m Manya Mahesh, a passionate visual storyteller with a focus on detail and emotion. Photography is not just an art form to me — it is a language through which I observe, understand, and express. Each frame I capture is a quiet moment, waiting to be seen, felt, and remembered. My work is inspired by the everyday, the unnoticed, and the beautifully ordinary.
